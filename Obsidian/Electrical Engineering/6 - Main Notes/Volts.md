# Volts

The unit of electrical potential is the Volt (V) where one volt is one joule per coulomb.

$$
Volts = \frac{watts}{amps} = \frac{joules/seconds}{amps} = \frac{joules}{amps/seconds} = \frac{joules}{coulombs}
$$
